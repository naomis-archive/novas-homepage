About Our Community

You don't have to be interested in coding to join us, we often have rounds of fun playing games, and talk about a variety of topics. Our moderator team, with Naomi, work with the community to set goals and provide projects that everyone can contribute to, in order to help build a sense of togetherness.

Gaming

A love for gaming is something a lot of people share, and we'd love to share it with you! Join us for activities like Among Us, Gartic Phone, Discord Activities, and more!.

We are ramping up our event schedule to generate fun and engaging things for everyone to enjoy.

Discussions

We love to have conversations about a variety of topics. Most of our discussion takes place in our Discord server, where we talk about things like philosophy, share our daily Wordle scores, and more!

If you're looking for a fun and inclusive place to chill out and vibe, you've found it!
